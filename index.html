About

"Every child deserves to see their festival on the page."

Supernova Artworks creates children's coloring and story books that celebrate the world's most beautiful cultural festivals. Founded by Novjean Kannathara, every book is designed to help children learn about traditions — from their own heritage or from cultures around the world — through the simple joy of coloring.
