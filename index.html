Hi, I'm Novjean

I create coloring and story books that celebrate the rich tapestry of world cultures — from Kerala's golden Vishu to Guatemala's vibrant Semana Santa to Thailand's splashing Songkran. Each book is designed to help children connect with their roots, discover new traditions, and fall in love with the beauty of diversity. Bold lines, big spaces, and a little bit of wonder on every page.
